Play the Game!

Press the button to queue for a game!

Rules

  1. You will not know who the other player in your game is.
  2. You will be asked to pick whether to cooperate or not with the other player.
  3. If you both choose to cooperate, you both earn 3 points.
  4. If you do not cooperate and your opponent cooperates, you will get 5 points and they will get 0 points.
  5. If you cooperate and your opponent doesn't cooperate, you will get 0 points and they will get 5 points.
  6. If you both choose not to cooperate, you both get 1 point
  7. This will repeat for 10 rounds, and then the game ends
  8. If you have created an account, your points earned will be added to your account's total, and your actions that game will be recorded.
