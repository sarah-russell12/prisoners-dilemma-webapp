Welcome!

The Prisoner's Dilemma is a game that examines how two people may or may not cooperate. The game presents a scenario where cooperation is in the best interest of the two players, however there is an incentive for both players to not cooperate.

The Dilemma's scenario is this: You and another player are criminals working together and got arrested. You are isolated from the other player. The prosecuters cannot convict both you and the other player on the major crime, but they can convict you both on secondary charges. The lesser charge carries a term of 1 year in prison. The major charge would put you in prison for 2 years. So if you were convicted on all charges, you would be in prison for the next 3 years. The prosecuters offer you and the other player this deal: testify against the other player and your minor charge would be dropped, meaning you serve no time in prison. However, your partner would get at least two years in prison.

The end results of this scenario are: If both players remain silent, they both serve 1 year in prison. If one player testifies and the other doesn't, or vice versa, the player who testifies walks free and the other is convicted on all charges and serves 3 years in prison. If both players testify against each other, they are both convicted on the major charge and have the minor charge dropped for their cooperation, meaning they both serve 2 years in prison.

This app does a verson of the Prisoner's Dilemma called the Iterated Prisoner's Dilemma. Where the players are given the same scenario a number of times, and have to choose whether to cooperate or not cooperate with each other each time. This will use a positive point system rather than a punishment system for simplicity.

Rules

  1. You will not know who the other player in your game is.
  2. You will be asked to pick whether to cooperate or not with the other player.
  3. If you both choose to cooperate, you both earn 3 points.
  4. If you choose not to cooperate and the other player does, you will get 5 points and they will get no points.
  5. If you choose to cooperate and the other player does not, they will get 5 points and you will get no points.
  6. If you both choose not to cooperate, you both get 1 point
  7. This will repeat for 10 rounds, and then the game ends
  8. If you have created an account, your points earned will be added to your account's total, and your actions that game will be recorded.
